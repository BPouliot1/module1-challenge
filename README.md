# module1-challenge
This project informs us of search engine optimization, online reputation management, and social media marketing. 
This is useful because  optimizing your search engine allows you to increase visiblity and helps find the right customers for your business. 
Online reputation management is beneficial because it gives you the control over what potential customers see when they search for your business.
Social media marketing determines which platform is the most suitable for your brand, all while using analytics to find the right markets and increase your lead generation.
